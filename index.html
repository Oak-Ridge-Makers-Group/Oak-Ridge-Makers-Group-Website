About Us

The Oak Ridge Makers Group is a community-driven makerspace dedicated to fostering creativity, learning, and collaboration. We provide access to tools, workspace, and a supportive community for makers of all skill levels.

Our mission is to democratize access to fabrication tools and knowledge, enabling individuals to transform their ideas into reality. Whether you're interested in woodworking, 3D printing, electronics, programming, or any other creative pursuit, you'll find the resources and community support you need to succeed.

We believe in the power of hands-on learning and collaborative problem-solving. Our diverse community includes hobbyists, professionals, students, and entrepreneurs who share knowledge, skills, and inspiration with one another.
